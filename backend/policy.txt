CUSTOMER SUPPORT QUALITY ASSURANCE POLICY

PURPOSE
This policy defines the standards and expectations for customer support interactions to ensure consistent, high-quality service, customer satisfaction, and compliance with company guidelines.
SCOPE
This policy applies to all customer support agents handling customer queries across all communication channels, including chat, email, and voice.
CORE PRINCIPLES

3.1 Professionalism

Agents must maintain a respectful, polite, and professional tone at all times.
Avoid slang, offensive language, or unprofessional expressions.

3.2 Accuracy

Provide correct and complete information based on company knowledge base.
Avoid guessing; if unsure, escalate or seek clarification.

3.3 Clarity

Responses should be clear, concise, and easy to understand.
Avoid jargon unless necessary, and explain when used.

3.4 Empathy

Acknowledge customer concerns and emotions.
Use empathetic language such as “I understand your concern” or “I’m sorry for the inconvenience.”

3.5 Resolution Focus

Aim to resolve issues in the first interaction whenever possible.
Provide actionable solutions and next steps.

3.6 Compliance

Follow all data privacy and security policies.
Do not share sensitive or confidential information.
RESPONSE STANDARDS

4.1 Greeting

Begin with a friendly and professional greeting.
Example: “Hello, thank you for contacting support.”

4.2 Understanding

Confirm the issue by paraphrasing the customer’s concern.

4.3 Solution Delivery

Provide step-by-step guidance where applicable.
Ensure instructions are complete and correct.

4.4 Closing

End with a courteous closing statement.
Example: “Please let me know if you need further assistance.”

PROHIBITED BEHAVIOR
Rude, dismissive, or argumentative responses.
Providing false or misleading information.
Ignoring customer queries or delaying responses unnecessarily.
Sharing internal or confidential company data.

ESCALATION GUIDELINES
Escalate complex or unresolved issues to the appropriate team.
Inform the customer about escalation and expected timelines.

QUALITY METRICS
Support interactions will be evaluated based on:
Tone and professionalism
Accuracy of information
Resolution effectiveness
Compliance with policy
Customer satisfaction

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT
Agents are expected to review feedback and improve performance through training and coaching.
